A story of a horrible day

04/09/2016 08:18:59

Today she’s 16
she’s still smiling
and i can’t take it
I keep crying
and you don’t know it
I remember those days
that I was happy with you
now I’m sad without you

my day is boring
my life is boring
everything becomes boring without love
and I'm still need you
your hugs, your lips
you...

I'm scared
my life doesn’t make no sense, no more
this poem also
my lips without their
this can’t be a mistake

I can’t smile today
but I can tomorrow
if this day you come to me
to be happy again.
